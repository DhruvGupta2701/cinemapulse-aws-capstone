Behind the Scenes

Building the infrastructure for real-time audience sentiment.

The Vision


CinemaPulse isn't just a database; it's a dynamic platform designed to bridge the gap between film studios and their audience.

During blockbuster releases, viewer feedback spikes unpredictably. Traditional analysis tools often crash under the load or provide data too late. CinemaPulse leverages AWS Cloud Native solutions to capture every reaction, rating, and review in real-time, ensuring producers get the "Director's Cut" of the data instantly.

Cloud Native Architecture

Flask Backend AWS EC2 RDS MySQL

Production Requirements

Hardware Required

  • Processor: Intel i5 or equivalent (Min)
  • RAM: 4GB (8GB recommended for MERN)
  • Storage: 128 GB SSD/HDD
  • Network: High-speed (10 Mbps+)
  • Audio/Visual: Projector & Mic system

Software Stack

  • Backend: Python & Flask
  • Database: Amazon RDS (MySQL)
  • Infrastructure: AWS EC2 (Elastic Compute)
  • Security: AWS IAM Role-Based Access
